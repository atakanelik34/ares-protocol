# Anti-Dump Policy (v2.1)

## Policy Boundary
**contractual obligations, not on-chain transfer restrictions.**

ARES anti-dump controls are implemented through legal/vesting policy and governance process, not global transfer hooks on ERC-20 transfers.

## Policy Instruments
1. Vesting-based supply release schedules
2. Contractual disposal covenants for allocated tranches
3. Governance disclosure standards for large-holder behavior
4. Market operations transparency (buyback/burn policy visibility)

## Why This Boundary Exists
- preserves DEX compatibility
- avoids brittle transfer-hook edge cases
- keeps token behavior predictable for integrations

## Mainnet-Prep Note
Dedicated vesting and enforcement modules remain a separate mainnet-prep sprint.
